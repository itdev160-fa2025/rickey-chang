Betta Fish

Different Types of Bettas

Bettas are a very beautiful and colorful type of fish. There are many types and is very noticeable within the males. For example, some have short tails and some have long tails. Each can have their own shade of color which are past down from the parents.

Names of Different Types

  • Half Moon
  • Koi
  • Blue Marble
  • Crown Tail
